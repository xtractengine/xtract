Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Xtract

What type of contracts does Xtract support?

+
Xtract is specifically built for TREC One to Four Family Residential Contract (Resale) — TREC NO. 20-18. It extracts all key fields including closing date, sales price, parties, broker information, earnest money, HOA, title policy, survey, option period, and more.

How long does extraction take?

+
Typically 15 to 30 seconds per contract. Results appear directly on your screen — no email, no waiting, no refresh. Simply upload, click Extract, and your structured data is ready.

Is my contract data secure?

+
Yes. Your contracts are processed securely and never stored on our servers. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Each client has a unique password and their data is completely isolated from other clients.

Can I connect my own Excel or Google Sheet?

+
Yes. Each extraction automatically adds a new row to your dedicated sheet inside our system. Additionally, you can optionally paste your own Google Sheet link and we will display it for quick access after each extraction.

What is File No. and is it required?

+
File No. is your internal reference number for the contract (e.g. 2026-001). It is completely optional. If you leave it blank, the system automatically assigns N/A. This number appears as the first column in your extraction history.

Do I need to log in every time?

+
No. Check the Remember Me box on your first login and your credentials are saved securely in your browser. Future visits go directly to the tool — just enter File No., upload PDF, and click Extract.

Can I request additional data fields?

+
Yes. Professional and Business plan clients can request additional columns to be added to their extraction output and Excel sheet. Contact us and we typically implement requests within 24-48 hours.

What happens when I reach my monthly limit?

+
You will see a clear message when your limit is reached. Contact us to upgrade your plan and we will increase your limit immediately. Limits reset at the start of each billing month.

How do I get started?

+
Contact us via the Contact page or email hello@xtract.io. We will set up your account, send you login credentials, and you can be extracting contracts within the same day. A 7-day free trial is available with no credit card required.

Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

By using Xtract, you agree to these data practices and limitations of liability. Please read carefully before uploading any documents.

1. No Data Retention Policy

The Service is built entirely on a Stateless, Zero-Retention Pipeline. We are committed to data privacy and compliance by design:

  • No Storage: We do not maintain databases to store, log, cache, or archive uploaded Word documents, generated PDFs, or generated RamQuest XML files.
  • In-Memory Processing: All document parsing occurs strictly in temporary server memory.
  • Instant Deletion: All uploaded files and translated outputs are completely and permanently purged from our servers the exact moment the XML payload is sent to your browser or within sixty (60) seconds of processing.

2. Third-Party Data Processing (Anthropic Claude API)

To provide instant document extraction, the Service utilizes the Anthropic Claude API.

  • Data sent via this pipeline is protected under Anthropic's commercial enterprise terms.
  • Your data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS 1.3 encryption.
  • Important: Under Anthropic's enterprise API policy, your data and documents are not stored by Anthropic, are not used to train AI models, and are processed completely confidentially.

The Service enforces strict in-transit encryption (AES-256 / TLS 1.3) to protect data moving between your browser and our processing pipeline. Because our platform is entirely stateless and does not act as a data repository, we maintain a minimized risk profile.
